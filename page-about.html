About FantasyNow+

FantasyNow+ was founded on a simple premise: fantasy football should be fun, not a full-time job.

We know how quickly the hobby can become overwhelming. Between tracking injury reports, analyzing complex player tiers, and trying to decipher contradictory expert advice, the joy of the game often gets lost in the data. We built FantasyNow+ to bridge that gap.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide the casual fantasy manager with professional-grade insights and automation tools that save time and deliver a competitive edge. We believe that you shouldn't need to spend hours in a spreadsheet to make smart, informed decisions for your team.

Built for You

Everything you see on this site—from our custom roster generators to our streamlined, tier-based ranking visualizations—is built with one person in mind: the manager who wants to dominate their league without sacrificing their entire weekend.

We are constantly iterating, building new tools, and finding ways to make fantasy management more intuitive. Thank you for being part of the FantasyNow+ community!
